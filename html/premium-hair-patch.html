The Science of Natural Hairlines

At Growig Hair Solution, located in the premium precinct of DLF Phase 2, Gurgaon, we redefine the standard of non-surgical hair replacement. A truly invisible hair patch is not merely a product; it is a meticulously engineered aesthetic enhancement tailored to the unique contours of your scalp and your natural hair growth patterns.

The structural integrity of a premium hair patch relies on the base material. We utilize hyper-realistic substrates, from ultra-thin poly membranes that mimic the epidermis to fine Swiss lace that offers unparalleled breathability. Each strand of 100% Remy human hair is individually hand-tied or injected into this base, replicating the natural direction and density of biological hair growth. This painstaking process ensures that the transition between the patch and your existing hair is imperceptible, even under close scrutiny or harsh lighting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a premium hair patch last? expand_more

With proper maintenance and high-quality care, a premium hair patch typically lasts between 3 to 6 months. Factors such as the base material (lace vs. thin skin) and your lifestyle will influence longevity. We provide comprehensive guidance on maximizing the lifespan of your system.

Can I shower, swim, and exercise with the patch? expand_more

Absolutely. Once the medical-grade adhesive has fully cured (usually 24 hours after fitting), the bond is highly secure and waterproof. You can maintain an active lifestyle, including intense workouts and swimming, with complete confidence.

Will people be able to tell I'm wearing a hair patch? expand_more

No. Our modern hair patches utilize ultra-thin bases and hand-tied, natural Remy hair. The transitional hairline is engineered to mimic natural growth, making it virtually undetectable to the naked eye or touch.

What maintenance is required? expand_more

Routine maintenance is required every 3 to 4 weeks. This involves safely removing the patch, thoroughly cleaning the scalp and the system, and reapplying fresh adhesive. Many clients opt to visit our studio for professional maintenance to ensure optimal hygiene and rebonding.
